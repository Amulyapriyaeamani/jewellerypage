About Us

Mangata and Gallo is a fine jewelry brand specializing in life’s most cherished moments—engagements, weddings, and anniversaries. Based in Austin, Texas, we primarily operate online while maintaining a charming local storefront with an attached atelier, where customers can experience our craftsmanship up close.

Our collection is renowned for its timeless elegance, high-quality materials, and classic designs that celebrate love and special occasions.

Meet the Designer

Mariana, the founder and lead designer of Mangata and Gallo, is an Austin native with a passion for jewelry artistry. After graduating with a specialization in diamond cutting and metalsmithing, she established the brand in her hometown.
